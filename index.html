Welcome to Little Bird Toys, where we've been crafting handcrafted wooden toys since our establishment in 2018. Our mission is to enhance playtime with toys that inspire imagination and promote meaningful connections. Designed for longevity and sustainability, our heirloom-quality collection is meticulously crafted from Baltic birch wood, ensuring durability and safety that meet ASTM and CA-Prop 65 standards. Based in Toms River, NJ, our workshop minimizes waste and supports local economy, embodying our commitment to eco-friendly practices. We invite you to visit our store and experience our charming play space, where you can meet our passionate team and discover the joy of simple, safe play with Little Bird Toys. Join our family and embrace playtime that lasts a lifetime.
